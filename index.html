About Us

We are renowned contracting company in Saudi Arabia under commercial registration number 1010335813 with the ministry of Commerce & Industry. The company has its Head office in Riyadh, with its address as P.O.Box-28062, Riyadh-11437.

The Company’s initial activity was sub-contracting of Electrical Installations In 2015, the company entered in to a JV (Joint Venture) with Overseas Constrution Company (Pvt) Ltd to expand its activities to become a broad-line contracting comapny. 

Today, SecuPower is a full fledged Contracting Engineering and Services and organization in Saudi Arabia.

Our Vision

SECUPOWER aspires to become an acknowledged leader in the fields of general contracting,construction,management, with the provision of engineering services in Saudi Arabia and beyond through the diversity and excellence of our people,providing value to our customers with a commitment for continuous improvement and development. 

Our Mission

Listen to our customers to understand their needs. formulate a proper solution & deliver the optimum to their benefit.
